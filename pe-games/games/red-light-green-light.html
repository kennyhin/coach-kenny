🚦 Run · Stop · Control

Red Light, Green Light

Grades K-2

📖 How to Play

  • All students line up at one end of the gym/field.
  • The teacher (or a student leader) stands at the opposite end facing away.
  • When the leader says "Green Light!" students walk, jog, or run toward the leader.
  • When the leader says "Red Light!" everyone must FREEZE immediately.
  • The leader turns around between commands. Anyone caught moving on "Red Light" goes back to start.
  • First student to reach the leader wins and becomes the new leader!

🎯 Variations

  • Yellow Light: Students must walk slowly (slow-motion movement).
  • Blue Light: Students must hop on one foot.
  • Dance Light: Students freeze in a silly dance pose.
  • Animal Lights: Green = horse gallop, Red = freeze like a statue, Yellow = turtle walk.

🧰 Equipment

  • Cones to mark start and finish lines
  • Optional: traffic light visual cards (red/green/yellow)
