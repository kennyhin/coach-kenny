Sharks & Minnows

Grades 1-3

📖 How to Play

  • One student is the "Shark" and stands in the middle of the gym/field.
  • All other students are "Minnows" lined up on one side.
  • On "Go!" minnows try to run across to the other side without being tagged.
  • Tagged minnows become Sharks for the next round.
  • Continue until everyone is caught, or play as many rounds as time allows.

🎯 Variations

  • Swimming Minnows: Minnows must use a "swimming" arm motion while running.
  • Safe Zones: Add mats or hula hoops as safe islands where minnows can rest.
  • Multi-Shark: Start with 2-3 sharks on harder rounds.
  • Animal Swim: Minnows must use a specific locomotor (gallop like a horse, crab walk, etc.).

🧰 Equipment

  • Boundary cones (6-8)
  • Wristbands or pinnies for sharks (optional)
Nevada PE Standards

NVPE S1.E2: Hop, gallop, slide, skip with balance and control.

NVPE S3.E2: Understands cardiorespiratory fitness concepts such as fast/slow heart rate.

NVPE S2.E1: Demonstrates spatial awareness by avoiding others in general space.
