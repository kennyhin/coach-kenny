Tag Games

Grades K-2

📖 How to Play

  • Choose 1-2 students to be "It" (the taggers).
  • All other students scatter within the playing area.
  • On "Go!" the taggers chase and try to tag others.
  • When tagged, a student becomes a new tagger.
  • Play for 1-2 minutes. Rotate taggers each round.

🎯 Variations

  • Freeze Tag: Tagged players freeze until a free player crawls under their legs.
  • Tunnel Tag: Tagged players stand with legs apart. Free players can unfreeze them by crawling through.
  • Line Tag: Taggers can only tag students who are also on a line on the court/field.
  • Balance Tag: While being chased, students must balance on one foot when crossing a line.
  • Compliment Tag: When tagged, say something nice to the person who tagged you!

🧰 Equipment

  • Cones to mark boundaries (4-8 cones)
  • Necklaces or wristbands to identify taggers (optional)
  • Whistle or music player

📏 Space Needed

Gym, blacktop, or field — approximately 30×30 feet minimum. Adjust size based on grade level and number of students.
