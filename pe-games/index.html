📋 Nevada PE Standards Reference

Standard 1 — Motor Skills & Movement

Students demonstrate competency in motor skills and movement patterns needed to perform physical activities.

  • S1.E1: Locomotor — walk, jog, run
  • S1.E2: Locomotor — hop, gallop, slide, skip
  • S1.E3: Jump & land with control
  • S1.E4: Balance & weight transfer
  • S1.E5: Manipulative — throw, catch, kick

Standard 2 — Movement Concepts

Students understand movement concepts, principles, and strategies as they apply to learning and performing physical activities.

  • S2.E1: Spatial awareness — personal/general space
  • S2.E2: Pathways, levels, directions
  • S2.E3: Speed, force, flow concepts

Standard 3 — Physical Fitness & Activity

Students participate in regular physical activity and understand the relationship between activity and health.

  • S3.E1: Participates in activity majority of class
  • S3.E2: Recognizes cardiorespiratory fitness
  • S3.E3: Identifies physiological indicators
  • S3.E4: Warm-up, workout, cool-down

Standard 4 — Responsible Behavior

Students exhibit responsible personal and social behavior that respects self and peers.

  • S4.E1: Listening & following directions
  • S4.E2: Works cooperatively with others
  • S4.E3: Safe practices in activity
  • S4.E4: Rules and fair play

Standard 5 — Health-Enhancing Value

Students recognize the value of physical activity for health, enjoyment, challenge, self-expression, and social interaction.

  • S5.E1: Activity is good for personal health
  • S5.E2: Identifies enjoyable activities
  • S5.E3: Positive social interactions
