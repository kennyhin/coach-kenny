LOCOMOTOR MOVEMENTS

Fundamental movement skills every K-5 student masters through PE.

🚶

Walking

Heel-to-toe, arms swinging naturally, eyes forward. The foundation of all movement.

All Grades
🏃

Jogging / Running

Light on your feet, bent knees, arms pumping. Build endurance and speed.

All Grades
🦘

Hopping

One-foot take-off and landing. Builds balance and leg strength.

K-2
🐴

Galloping

One foot leads, the other follows. A smooth, rhythmic pattern.

K-3
⛸️

Sliding

Side-to-side movement, feet never cross. Quick direction changes.

K-3
🪴

Skipping

Step-hop, step-hop. A bouncy, fun pattern combining walk and hop.

1-3
🐸

Leaping

Take off on one foot, land on the other. Long, stretched flight.

2-5
🤸

Jumping

Two-foot takeoff and landing. Push off hard, land soft with bent knees.

All Grades
🦀

Crab Walk / Bear Walk

Non-locomotor fun that builds coordination and body awareness.

K-2
🐰

Bunny Hops

Both feet together, hopping forward. Great for leg power!

K-2
🎒

Dodging

Quick body shifts to avoid others. Essential for all sports.

2-5
📦

Crawling / Rolling

Space awareness and coordination. Log rolls, bear crawls, commando rolls.

K-3

NEVADA PE STANDARDS

All SLAM! PE activities align with the Nevada Academic Content Standards for Physical Education (NVACS).

S1

Motor Skills & Movement Patterns

Demonstrates competency in motor skills and movement patterns needed to perform a variety of physical activities.

S1.E1 – Locomotor (walk, run, hop, jump) S1.E2 – Non-locomotor (bend, stretch, twist) S1.E3 – Manipulative (throw, catch, kick)
S2

Movement Concepts & Principles

Understands movement concepts, principles, and strategies as they apply to physical activities.

S2.E1 – Spatial awareness S2.E2 – Pathways, levels, direction S2.E3 – Speed, force, flow
S3

Physical Activity & Fitness

Participates in physical activities and understands the impact on health and fitness.

S3.E1 – Active participation S3.E2 – Cardiorespiratory fitness S3.E3 – Flexibility & strength
S4

Responsible Personal & Social Behavior

Exhibits responsible personal and social behavior in physical activity settings.

S4.E1 – Follows rules & directions S4.E2 – Works cooperatively S4.E3 – Sportsmanship
S5

Value of Physical Activity

Recognizes the value of physical activity for health, enjoyment, challenge, self-expression, and social interaction.

S5.E1 – Self-expression & enjoyment S5.E2 – Challenge & goal-setting S5.E3 – Social interaction
