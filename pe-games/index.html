📋 Nevada PE Standards

Standards covered across all games (NVACS Physical Education)

Standard 1 — Movement & Locomotor Skills

Students demonstrate competency in motor skills and movement patterns needed to perform a variety of physical activities.

  • S1.E1: Locomotor — Walk, jog, run with appropriate form
  • S1.E2: Locomotor — Hop, gallop, slide, skip with balance
  • S1.E3: Locomotor — Jump and land in both horizontal and vertical planes
  • S1.E4: Locomotor — Jump rope basic and intermediate skills (Gr 3-5)
  • S1.E5: Locomotor — Demonstrates mature patterns in locomotor skills (Gr 3-5)
  • S1.E6: Non-locomotor — Balance, twist, spin, and bend (Gr 3-5)

Standard 2 — Movement Concepts

Students understand movement concepts, principles, and strategies as they apply to learning and performance.

  • S2.E1: Spatial Awareness — Moving in personal and general space
  • S2.E2: Pathways — Straight, curved, and zigzag movement patterns
  • S2.E3: Levels — High, medium, low movement levels
  • S2.E4: Speed & Force — Fast/slight velocity, strong/light force

Standard 3 — Physical Fitness & Activity

Students participate in regular physical activity and understand the relationship between activity and health.

  • S3.E1: Participates in physical activity a majority of class time
  • S3.E2: Recognizes cardiorespiratory fitness concepts
  • S3.E3: Identifies physiological indicators of exercise
  • S3.E4: Understands warm-up, workout, cool-down phases

Standard 4 — Responsible Behavior

Students exhibit responsible personal and social behavior that respects self and peers.

  • S4.E1: Demonstrates listening/following directions
  • S4.E2: Works cooperatively with others
  • S4.E3: Displays safe practices in activity settings
  • S4.E4: Applies rules and fair play

Standard 5 — Health-Enhancing Value

Students recognize the value of physical activity for health, enjoyment, challenge, self-expression, and social interaction.

  • S5.E1: Recognizes physical activity is good for personal health
  • S5.E2: Identifies activities that bring personal enjoyment
  • S5.E3: Describes positive social interactions in physical activity
